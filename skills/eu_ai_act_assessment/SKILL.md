# Skill: EU AI Act Assessment

## Purpose

Produce an EU AI Act-aligned assessment for AI-assisted cyber triage.

## Inputs

- detected cyber signals
- retrieved policy context
- intended system role

## Required Assessment Points

- AI-assisted decision-support role
- autonomous action allowance
- human oversight requirement
- prohibited-use check
- transparency note
- logging requirement

## Output

Structured JSON assessment.

## Safety Rules

- Do not claim legal certification.
- Do not allow autonomous impactful action.
- Require human oversight before containment or infrastructure changes.
